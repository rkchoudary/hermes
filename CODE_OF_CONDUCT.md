# Code of Conduct

Hermes is open-source infrastructure for regulated-industry AI development. The contributors and users include people whose day jobs involve audit, compliance, security, and high-stakes engineering. We expect everyone interacting in this project to be respectful of each other's expertise and time.

## Our standards

**Behavior we welcome:**
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when we make mistakes
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Behavior we won't tolerate:**
- The use of sexualized language or imagery, or sexual attention/advances of any kind
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments and personal/political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (physical address, email, etc.) without explicit permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, Discord/Slack if any) and in public spaces when an individual is officially representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report violations to **rkchoudary@users.noreply.github.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Reporter privacy is protected. We will not name reporters without their consent.

## Enforcement guidelines

Project maintainers will determine the appropriate consequences for any action they deem in violation:

1. **Correction** — A private written warning explaining what was wrong.
2. **Warning** — A public warning, with consequences if behavior continues.
3. **Temporary ban** — Temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time.
4. **Permanent ban** — Permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
